How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by filling out the form above, calling our hotline, or visiting our hospital directly.

Please bring your National ID, any previous medical records, test reports, and current medication list.

Yes, we accept various health insurance plans. Please contact us for specific insurance inquiries.

Yes, free parking is available for patients and visitors at our hospital premises.

Consultation fees vary by department and specialist. Please call our reception for current fee information.
